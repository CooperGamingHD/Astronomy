Controls

  • Shoot: B
  • Ground Pass: A or
  • Through Ball: Y
  • Lofted Pass: X
  • Dribble/Run: Move LS
  • Sprint: RT + Move LS
  • LB + Y: Lofted Through Pass
  • RB + A: Driven Ground Pass
  • Hold B then Tap B: Driven Shot
  • LB + B: Chip Shot
  • RB + B: Finesse Shot

The Most Effective Formations

  • In my opinion, any of the 4-3-3 formations work well in Fifa 17. Any of the 4-4-2, 3-4-3 and 4-3-2-1 will work well.
